Our Owners

Craig created North Lakes Insurance Agency in 2012 as a partner of Atlas Insurance Brokers to help his clients match their insurance risks to an insurance company with the right product, underwriting, and price. Craig has been a licensed insurance agent since 1987, working with Liberty Mutual and Sentry Insurance. Craig is a member of the Forest Lake Area Chamber of Commerce and currently serves on the Board of Linwood School Forest. Craig passionately studies wildlife of the Upper Great Lakes, enjoying canoeing, camping, and fishing wilderness lakes and rivers of Minnesota and Wisconsin.
