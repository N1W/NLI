Liability umbrella insurance is a low cost option that typically doubles or triples the amount of protection you have on all underlying insurance policies. Umbrella insurance works with your other insurance policies to provide additional protection. For example, most auto and home insurance policies provide a maximum of $500,000 in liability coverage but one umbrella policy can increase the amount of protection from each to a total of $1,500,000.
